About Us

Area 52 was thought up by Joseph Berrigan on September 14th 2021, 8 days after his birthday Joseph loves the idea of space exploration and aliens, so when he had to think of an idea for an amusement park for a school assignment, he created the concept of Area 52 rather quickly.

Area 52 is located in Statesboro, Georgia, because Joseph's favorite water park growing up was Splash in the Boro which is located in the same city. The diversity of Georgia's land was another reason Joseph chose to construct Area 52 in georgia he believed people travelling to the park would enjoy the natural scenery during the drive. Area 52 consists of many different attractions but the park has 5 main attractions which you can read about in the Rides section of our website.
