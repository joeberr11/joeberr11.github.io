Park Rides & Attractions

The park has many exciting attractions such as rollercoasters and bumper cars.

Wormholes

This galaxy themed mini golf is for all ages with twist and turns full of extraterrerial suprises. There are ups and downs and tons of alien obstacles such as spaceships and asteroids.


Milky Maze

The Milky Maze is a haunted house designed to be out of this world! You'll find yourself running from aliens pursuing you on their spaceships. Even though you're in space, we'll still hear your screams.


Blaster Tag

Blaster Tag is classic laser tag pitting aliens against humans. The alien team will receive blasters while the human team defend themselves with space rifles.


The Black Hole

You'll find yourself sucked into complete darkness with unexpected monsterous drops and twists into deep space. Bring your swim suits becaue this Black Hole leads to sea.


Hyper Drive

You'll go light speed on our signature rollarcoaster ride. Strap in as you teleport through the universe while dodging comets and meteoroids.
